Eco-Friendly Women's Knit Hats by Sunkhari

Alisha Bhattarai, the creative soul behind Sunkhari, recently moved from the vibrant streets of Nepal to the USA. A maker at heart, Alisha loves nothing more than knitting and dreaming up designs for cozy, eco-friendly hats and accessories. Whether she’s experimenting with new patterns or enjoying a quiet moment with her knitting needles and a cup of chai, her passion for crafting shines through in everything she creates.

At Sunkhari, we focus on using natural, renewable materials and thoughtful production methods because Alisha believes fashion should feel as good as it looks. Each piece is a blend of playful style and everyday comfort, designed to bring a little warmth and happiness to your day. With every hat or accessory, you’re not just wearing something handmade; you’re sharing in the positive energy that is put into every Sunkhari product.
